Overview of Metroshuttle

Metro-shuttle is a children's transportation app designed to alleviate parental concerns by providing a secure, user-friendly platform for real-time tracking, timely notifications, and peace of mind.

Metroshuttle also focuses on safety: Metro-shuttle enhances child safety by offering real-time GPS tracking, instant alerts for stops and arrivals, and the ability to communicate directly with the driver.
Metro-shuttle also focuses on convenience: Metro-shuttle simplifies the school commute by providing parents with accurate arrival times, route visualization, and the option to schedule rides in advance.
Metro-shuttle is focused on community: Metro-shuttle fosters a sense of community by connecting parents and drivers, facilitating communication and building trust.

Addressing the need for child safety in transportation

Parents often face uncertainty about their child's whereabouts during commutes. Issues such as delays, missed pickups, and lack of communication can cause stress and safety concerns. The need for a reliable, transparent, and efficient system to monitor children's transportation is paramount.

Key features and benefits

The app offers:
1. Live Tracking A live map view to monitor the shuttleâ€™s location and estimated arrival times.
2. Secure Check-In/Check-Out: Ensuring only authorized personnel can pick up and drop off children. (Giving access to only school coordinators)
3. Notifications/ Alerts: Allowing parents to receive updates on when their children have been picked up and dropped off.
4. Detailed Trip History: Access to a complete history of trips for security and convenience.
By integrating these features, Metro-shuttle provides a comprehensive solution that ensures the safety, security, and peace of mind for parents regarding their child's transportation.

Parent Features

User registration and profile setup

To ensure the safety and efficiency of your child's transportation, Metroshuttle requires a simple registration process. By creating an account, you'll gain access to real-time tracking, instant notifications, and the ability to manage your child's rides with ease.
Metroshuttle allows parents/ guardians create their personal profile that they use to set information about a child, including their school, pick-up and drop-off locations, and emergency contacts. This helps us match a child with the correct driver and ensures timely notifications.
